Film Editing

Temporality

Shot duration: How long a shot lasts in either seconds or frames.

Pace: Pace is the general shot duration in a scene, the quicker the cuts, the quicker the editing pace.

Long take: Shot with a long duration (how long?)

Plot time: The amount of time passed in the plot (I DON'T KNOW HOW THIS IS DIFF THAN STORY TIME)

Story time: The measurement of how much time has passed in the diagetic world of the story. The screen time of the shots could last only for a minute move through many years of story time.

Screen time: The measurement of how much time a scene lasts on screen. Usually used in reference or comparison to story/plot time.
