Non-linear notetaking helps to build information landscapes

When taking notes, a process I have began to try is to not write sentences but place some key ideas and words at different points on the page. Then as you take notes, begin to connect some of these terms, find the bridges between them, think how does X affect Y. Conceptual space is like a landscape, and this process helps build the landscape on which to remember some of the more finer-grained details.
